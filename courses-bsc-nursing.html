B.Sc Nursing

The Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Nursing is an undergraduate healthcare course that involves the study of the care of other individuals to ensure good health. The aim of the course is to create skilled nursing and paramedical staff. The syllabus highlights important academic concepts like human anatomy, nutrition, microbiology, genetics, physiology, biochemistry, psychology, pathology, pharmacology, and diagnosis; and essential holistic values like compassion, empathy, understanding, and care towards the patients. Registration under The Indian Nursing Council is mandatory to practice Nursing in India.

Eligiblity Criteria

For the B.Sc course, 10+2 in any stream from a recognized board. Candidates appearing for the 12th exam can also apply for Merit secured in the qualifying exam from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. For foreign aspirants, candidates must have completed 12 years of education securing at least 40% marks in the qualifying examination from a recognized board.

B.Sc Nursing Syllabus

Our syllabus outlines the academic roadmap for your courses, providing clear objectives and expectations.

Sem 1

Communicative English,Applied Anatomy,Applied Physiology,Applied Sociology,Applied Psychology,Nursing Foundations I
Sem 2

Applied Biochemistry ,Applied Nutrition & Dietetics ,Nursing Foundations II, Health/Nursing Informatics & Technology
Sem 3

Applied Microbiology and Infection control including Safety,Pharmacology I ,Pathology I ,Adult Health Nursing I with integrated Pathophysiology
Sem 4

Pharmacology II,PathologyII & Genetics,Adult Health Nursing II with integrated pathophysiology inclusing Geriatrics,Professionalism, Professional values & Ethics including bioethics
Sem 5

Child Health Nursing I,Mental Health Nursing I,Community Health Nursing I(including Environmental Science & Epidemiology),Educational Technology/Nursing Education,Introduction to Forensic Nursing and Indian Laws
Sem 6

Child Health Nursing II,Mental Health Nursing II,Nursing Management & LeadershipMidwifery/Obstetrics & Gynaecology I
Sem 7

Community Health Nursing II,Nursing Research and Statistics,Midwifery/Obstetrics & Gynaecology II
Sem 8

Internship(Intensive Practicum/ Residency Posting)
