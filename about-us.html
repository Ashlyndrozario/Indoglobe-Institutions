About
Indoglobe Institutions

We are one of the Leading and most Popular Nursing Educational Institutions from Karnataka with the aim to provide standard nursing education. The nursing profession is considered a caring profession to begin with, it is an art, science, and a vocation. We have established our INDOGLOBE INSTITUTIONS in the year 2020 and PAVANA EDUCATIONAL TRUST established in the year 2020. In our institution, We have GNM (Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery) and BSc (N) Bachelor of Sciences in Nursing PB. BSc (N) Post Basic Bachelor of Sciences in Nursing, MSc (N) Master of Sciences in Nursing, affiliated with KNC, INC, AND RGUHS.

Recognition by the Government of Karnataka

Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS)

Approval of Indian Nursing Council (INC)

Approval of the Karnataka State Diploma in Nursing Examination Board (KSDNEB)
